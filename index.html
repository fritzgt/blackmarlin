Introducing The Black Marlin EF-1

Meet the E-Foil of the future, using state of the art technology and out of the box thinking our team of engineers have advanced the current versions of E-Foils and have come up with our EF-1 smart E-Foil. Not only in the technology behind it but the artistic design this board has been meticulously constructed in order to achieve the sleekest, slimmest, fastest, smartest e-foil on the market.

A closer look
at the BlackMarlin EF-1

EF-1's brushless motors are designed and manufactured by the absolute best engineers in the industry. We have achieved what no competitor has and created a proprietary, maintenance free, direct drive motor–free of cumbersome gears.

The EF-1's propellers are precision machined out of a solid block of aluminum. We hand polish and balance each prop to perfection before finishing with a hard anodization.
EF-1's propellers are where the “rubber meets the road”, engineers will recognize that they are a work of art, and riders will appreciate that they extremely reliable.

Our protective nozzle has been redefined to not only offer safety and peace of mind to the rider, but improved hydrodynamics and added stability to the overall ride. With this new propulsion setup, Lift truly offers an experience like none other.

Our Story

We are a group of ocean loving, adventure seeking, adrenaline junkies and techies. We set out to create the most epic e-foil possible with a clear vision of what we saw was missing in the industry. We noticed all the current models offered by our competition were very similar in design and function. They all looked like big boogie boards, thick and bulky, not as appealing to the eye. They also used the same technology with similar performance and battery life, which we weren’t too impressed with. The way we see E-Foils is that they are the boards of the future, which is why we set out to create the slimmest, sleekest, fastest, longest range, smartest E-Foil in the industry.

Working and developing the Black Marlin EF-1 since the start of 2021 we had our first prototype built but the facility burned down due to an electrical fire in a neighboring warehouse space. Since then we were forced to go back to the drawing board but because of this we were able to analyze all the progress we had made and come up with additional improvements to the Black Marlin EF-1.
